Precedent Studies

Lessons from urban food systems around the world

Urban Agriculture Models

Detroit Urban Farms

Large-scale community gardens and urban farming initiatives transforming vacant land into productive food sources and community gathering spaces.

Singapore's "30 by 30" Initiative

Ambitious plan to produce 30% of the nation's food by 2030 through vertical farming, aquaponics, and innovative urban agriculture technologies.

Food Distribution Systems

Paris Local Food Networks

Interconnected short-supply chains connecting regional farmers to urban consumers, reducing transportation emissions and supporting local economies.

Copenhagen's Food District

Strategic concentration of food production, processing, and retail in redeveloped industrial areas creating jobs and community food security.

Policy and Governance

Examination of zoning reforms, incentive programs, and community-led initiatives that have successfully advanced urban food resilience in other cities.
